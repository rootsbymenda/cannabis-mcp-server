# Cannabis Regulatory Intelligence MCP Server

Real-time cannabis compliance data across US states, Canada, and international scheduling frameworks.

## Tools

### `check_cannabis_testing`
State-by-state testing limits for pesticides, heavy metals, microbials, residual solvents, mycotoxins, and potency requirements. Covers all regulated US states.

### `check_controlled_substance`
Cross-reference against UN INCB Yellow List (154 scheduled narcotic drugs), EU Drug Precursors Regulation (46 controlled chemicals), and EMCDDA Novel Psychoactive Substances database (41 risk-assessed NPS).

### `check_cannabis_compliance`
Jurisdiction-specific compliance requirements including US state regulations and Health Canada cannabis rules (383 Canadian regulations).

### `search_cannabis_regulations`
Full-text search across all cannabis regulatory databases — testing limits, controlled substances, compliance rules, and international scheduling.
